About me:

I am currently a postdoc in the group Random matrices, Stochastic Models and Analysis at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. My research interests are in Random Matrix Theory and Free Probability.

I have notably been working on a strategy to interpolate random matrices with free operators, this is the main technical tool of several of the papers listed below. You can find here an elementary introduction to the topic.

Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2021 from ENS Lyon and Kyoto University, under the cosupervision of Alice Guionnet and Benoît Collins. I received my master degree in mathematics from ENS Lyon in 2017.
